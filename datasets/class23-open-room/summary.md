# Class23 Open-Room Hand Hygiene Dataset (2024, restricted)

105 untrimmed videos from 23 participants washing hands in a food lab across three camera setups. Captured at 1080p/30FPS; used for open-room action recognition/detection. Not publicly released due to consent restrictions.

- Data type: multi-view RGB video (untrimmed sessions).
- Labels: handwashing actions/steps for open-room recognition and detection tasks.
- Availability: data restricted/non-public; no code or pretrained models provided here.
