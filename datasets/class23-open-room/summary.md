# Class23 Open-Room Hand Hygiene Dataset (2024, restricted)

Open-room, side-view hand-hygiene dataset with 105 untrimmed videos from 23 participants across three camera viewpoints (1080p/30 FPS).

## Overview
- **Size:** 105 untrimmed videos recorded over 5 class sessions; 3 camera views.
- **Participants:** 23 students (Purdue University Class-23 cohort).
- **Modalities:** RGB video; side-view cameras.
- **Labels:** per-frame action labels; includes non-hygiene actions and occlusion.

## Structure
- **Cameras:** Room1 Camera1, Room2 Camera1, Room2 Camera2.
- **Action sets:** 4 core hand-hygiene actions + non-hygiene actions (paper towel, swinging hands, occlusion).
- **Splits:** trimmed clips for training/validation; untrimmed videos for detection testing.

## Availability
- **Access:** restricted / not publicly released; contact authors.

## Notes
- **Use cases:** open-room action detection and cross-scenario generalization.
