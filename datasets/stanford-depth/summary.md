# Stanford Depth Hand Hygiene Dataset (2015, non-public)

Depth-only hospital dataset for dispenser-use and hand-presence detection.

## Overview
- **Size:** ~20 hours of depth video from overhead sensors.
- **Modalities:** depth frames (privacy-preserving).
- **Labels:** hand presence and dispenser-use events.

## Structure
- **Packaging:** not publicly released; described in the 2015 Stanford PAIR paper.

## Availability
- **Access:** non-public; contact authors.

## Notes
- **Use cases:** depth-based compliance monitoring and dispenser interaction.
