# OCDetect Wrist Motion Dataset (2023)

Smartwatch motion dataset for detecting compulsive handwashing behaviors. 22 participants over 28 days (~3,000 handwashing events). Focuses on event detection rather than WHO step coverage.

- Data type: IMU wrist-worn time-series over daily life.
- Labels: event-level handwashing annotations (no WHO step labels).
- Availability: data public on Zenodo; no bundled code/weights here.

## Download
- Zenodo: `https://zenodo.org/records/13924901`

## Notes
- Useful for anomaly detection or general handwash/no-handwash classification; no step-level labels.
