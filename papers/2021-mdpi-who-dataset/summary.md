# Hand-Washing Video Dataset Annotated According to the WHO Guidelines (Data 2021)

Presents the PSKUS hospital dataset with 3,185 annotated episodes and detailed statistics.

- Media/architecture: video dataset only; no model architecture proposed.
- Contribution: defines 6 WHO movement classes (+ Other) with per-frame labels and dataset statistics to enable step-wise research.
- Availability: data public on Zenodo; code/weights not central in the paper; no external API.
